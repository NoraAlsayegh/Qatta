# Qatta (القطة)

## About

Qatta is a bilingual expense-sharing and bill-splitting platform designed for Arabic and English users.

The idea came from a simple problem many people face when sharing expenses with friends, roommates, travel groups, or family members. Keeping track of who paid, who owes money, and settling payments can quickly become confusing.

Qatta helps users manage shared expenses in a simple and organized way through a user-friendly experience that supports both Arabic and English.

## Problem

Many people struggle to keep track of shared expenses and balances within groups. Existing solutions often do not provide a seamless experience for both Arabic and English users.

## Solution

Qatta allows users to:

- Split expenses with friends and groups
- Track balances and payments
- Manage shared expenses in one place
- Use the platform in both Arabic and English

## Current Stage

Qatta is currently in the prototype stage with interactive web and mobile prototypes completed.
